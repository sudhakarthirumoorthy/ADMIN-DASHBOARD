Frequently Asked Questions

How can I reset my password?

You can reset your password by clicking on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page.

What should I do if I encounter a system error?

If you face any system errors, please contact our support team via the contact form.

How do I add a new doctor?

You can add a new doctor by navigating to the Doctors section and clicking on 'Add Doctor'.

Where can I view the hospital schedule?

The hospital schedule can be viewed in the 'Schedule' section on the dashboard.
